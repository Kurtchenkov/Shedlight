Shedlight Business Plan

Executive Summary

Company Name: Shedlight

Founded: [Year]

Founder: Kurt Linnett

Mission Statement: Shedlight exists to disciple individuals, families, and professionals in the teachings of Jesus Christ, helping them navigate life's challenges with biblical principles, and empowering them to fulfil their God-given purpose.

Vision Statement: To see individuals, families, and communities transformed by the power of discipleship, grounded in Scripture, and actively living out their faith in their personal and professional lives.

Goals:

  • Equip new believers with practical tools for spiritual growth.
  • Empower parents to raise their children in the ways of the Lord.
  • Provide discipleship for professionals in the workplace, helping them integrate faith with their careers.

Company Overview

Shedlight is a discipleship organization rooted in biblical principles, offering courses, workshops, and mentorship programmes designed to help individuals grow in their walk with Christ. Our unique focus is on personal discipleship for new Christians, family discipleship, and workplace mentoring. Shedlight combines deep faith with practical, everyday guidance for living out the teachings of Jesus Christ.

Core Services:

  • Discipleship Programmes: Guided teachings for new believers to strengthen their faith.
  • Parenting Workshops: Helping parents become disciple-makers in their own homes.
  • Workplace Discipleship: Integrating biblical principles with professional development.
  • Online Community: Building a supportive, Christ-centred community through platforms like Discord and Ko-fi.

Market Research

Target Audience:

  • New Christians looking for spiritual guidance as they begin their journey with Christ.
  • Christian parents seeking to raise their children according to biblical principles.
  • Christian professionals who want to integrate their faith into their careers.

Market Trends:

  • Growing demand for digital discipleship and online faith-based communities.
  • Parents are looking for resources to raise children with strong values in a secular society.
  • Christian professionals seek guidance on living out their faith in the workplace.

Organization and Management

Founder: Kurt Linnett – With a background in ministry and discipleship, Kurt brings decades of experience in mentoring individuals, families, and communities in biblical living.

Team:

  • Programme Coordinators: Focus on developing course content and discipleship material.
  • Mentors: Provide one-on-one guidance to participants.
  • Community Managers: Oversee online platforms and ensure participant engagement.

Services and Products

Core Programmes:

  • Discipleship for New Believers: Online courses, daily devotions, and mentorship programmes.
  • Parenting Disciple-Makers Course: Workshops and online resources for parents to raise children in the ways of the Lord.
  • Workplace Discipleship Programme: Integrating faith into professional life with courses on ethics and leadership.
  • Community Engagement: Ongoing support through Discord and Ko-fi platforms with weekly Q&A sessions and devotionals.

Marketing Strategy

Positioning: Shedlight offers a holistic approach to discipleship catering to individuals, families, and professionals. We provide both digital and face-to-face discipleship opportunities with a focus on long-term spiritual growth.

Marketing Channels:

  • Social Media (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube) to engage followers.
  • Email Marketing with newsletters and course updates.
  • Partnerships with local churches and Christian networks.
  • Referral Programme to encourage participants to refer others.

Content Strategy:

  • Create content addressing real-life issues such as faith in the workplace and raising children with biblical values.
  • Use personal stories and testimonies to build credibility.
  • Offer free resources like e-books and devotionals to attract new participants.

Financial Plan

Revenue Streams:

  • Courses and Workshops
  • Subscription Model
  • Donations
  • Merchandise (books, resources, branded items)

Expenses:

  • Salaries, Marketing, Technology, and Operational Costs

Social Impact

Shedlight’s mission extends beyond individual transformation. By equipping Christians to live out biblical principles, Shedlight helps create stronger families, workplaces, and communities grounded in faith. This results in a lasting, positive impact on society as a whole.

Conclusion

Shedlight is uniquely positioned to meet the growing need for spiritual direction, authentic community, and practical discipleship. With a clear mission and focus, Shedlight will empower individuals, families, and professionals to live as faithful disciples of Jesus Christ.
