Shedlight:
Shedding light on purpose

Shedlight exists to help both new and seasoned Christians move beyond simply attending church—to actually living the life Jesus promised. Whether at home, at school, at work, or in social settings, our focus is on real, purposeful discipleship. We're not just about helping people live life—we’re here to help them live the life God has already planned for them.

The Covenant of Fire
Abraham the Father of Us All

The Covenant of Fire is a sweeping reimagining of the life of Abraham, the patriarch of nations. Blending historical accuracy with rich storytelling, this novel delves into the emotional and physical trials of a man who dared to follow a call into the unknown. From the burning ruins of Ur to the promised lands of Canaan, this is a story of faith forged in fire, a family divided by ambition and betrayal, and a covenant that would shape the course of history.

For readers of historical fiction, and biblical epics, and those who long to explore the faith and trials of one of history’s most enigmatic figures, The Covenant of Fire offers a vivid and powerful retelling of an ancient journey that still resonates today.

Grace-Filled Parenting
Navigating the Pitfalls of Good Intentions

Through practical advice, scriptural principles, and reflective questions, Linnett challenges parents to move beyond control and perfectionism toward a grace-filled model of parenting rooted in love, empathy, and faith. Covering everything from discipline and emotional intelligence to resilience, creativity, and spiritual growth, this book empowers parents to cultivate homes where children feel secure, valued, and inspired to thrive.

Whether you’re a new parent or navigating the teen years, Grace-Filled Parenting offers a thoughtful roadmap for growing both your children—and yourself—in God’s grace.

Rediscover Authority
A Journey of Redemption and Empowerment

"Rediscovering Authority" is a profound exploration of uncovering timeless truths about humanity's role as stewards of God's creation. Delving into the historical, theological, and practical aspects of stewardship, this ebook illuminates the divine mandate and authority entrusted to humans. Through insightful reflections, readers gain a deeper understanding of their identity and purpose, equipped with a comprehensive study guide featuring key concepts, scripture reflections, and discussion questions. This transformative journey empowers readers to fulfill their stewardship with confidence and reverence, making it an invaluable resource for individuals, study groups, and ministries embracing God's design for creation and stewardship.
